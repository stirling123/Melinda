Melinda Blackman

BMus(Hons) LRSM

Melinda is dedicated to passing on her skills as a musician. As a teacher for Richmond Music Trust and the Colchester and Chelmsford Allegro Academy, Melinda has taught and led music workshops in many primary and secondary schools throughout London and Essex. She also coaches chamber music groups for Procorda's Young String Experience and has been involved in many residential summer music courses for the Ingenium Academy and Yorkshire Young Sinfonia.

"A varied recital with engaging communication and reliable technical control. The phrasing was beguiling and technical challenges were met fluently" LRSM Examiner
